Our Story, Our Passion

From a small repair shop to a leading cycle retailer, we are driven by the joy of riding.

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1998 by cycling enthusiast Ramesh Surana, Surana Cycles started as a modest local service center. Ramesh's dedication to **quality service and community building** quickly established us as the go-to place for riders. Over the last two decades, we have grown into a multi-location retailer, but our commitment to the local cycling community remains our core focus.
