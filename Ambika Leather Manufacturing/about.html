About Ambika Leather Manufacturing

Our story

Founded in 1998 in Rajasthan, Ambika Leather Manufacturing began as a small family workshop focused on artisanal leather goods. Over the decades we've combined traditional handcrafting techniques with modern design to produce durable, timeless pieces. Every product is inspected by our master craftsmen and comes with a repair promise.

What we make

  • Bags — backpacks, satchels, messenger bags
  • Purses & wallets — slim, continental, coin
  • Jackets — biker, bomber, tailored
  • Accessories — belts, keychains, watch straps

Sustainability

We source responsibly tanned leather and run water-efficient processes. Unwanted scraps are repurposed into accessories to minimize waste.

Why choose us?

  1. Handcrafted quality and attention to detail.
  2. Lifetime repair promise on stitching and hardware.
  3. Transparent pricing and ethical sourcing.
  4. Custom orders available on request.

Team & Workshop

Our team of 45 skilled artisans work in our 2000 sq ft workshop. We train local youth in leathercraft skills and pay fair wages.
